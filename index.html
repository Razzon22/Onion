Cooking with Onions

Onions have been used since 5000 BC, and
they have been popular for eating and cooking ever since.

Value in Cooking

We all know that onions can make us cry when we cut them raw. But after they're cooked, they stop making our eyes water and start making our mouth water! After onions are cooked, they can give a sweetish, mellow flavor. Sometimes you don't recognize that onions are in a dish, but once they're taken out, it's easy to notice something's missing.

There are some people that cook and eat just the onion, but it's much more common when people use them cooking a recipe. There are many fancy dishes around the world that use onions to add that extra taste, and some dishes use onions as the main ingredient. Here are just a couple of the many things that people can make with onions.
